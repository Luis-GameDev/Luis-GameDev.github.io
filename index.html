A$gang Bot

A$gang Bot is a Discord community bot built with Discord.JS v14, designed to provide entertainment and foster a friendly community atmosphere. Its core features include:

  • True or Jail: A daily question feature where members answer fun or intriguing questions to engage the community.
  • /hate Commands: Players can humorously express their "hate" for specific people, sparking entertaining interactions.
  • Story Game: A creative mini-game where members collaboratively write stories by adding one word at a time, resulting in amusing and imaginative tales.

The bot combines playful elements with interaction, creating an active and enjoyable community environment.
